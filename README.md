## What is a Nether Portal Calculator

A Nether Portal Calculator is a simple tool that converts coordinates between the Overworld and the Nether.

In Minecraft, the distance ratio between these two dimensions is:

- 1 block in the Nether = 8 blocks in the Overworld

So instead of guessing, the calculator gives you exact coordinates for placing your portal.

## How It Works

The concept is very simple.

If you are in the Overworld:
- Divide your X and Z coordinates by 8  

If you are in the Nether:
- Multiply your X and Z coordinates by 8  

The Y coordinate (height) usually stays the same.

## Example

Let’s say your Overworld coordinates are:

X: 800  
Z: 400  

To find the Nether location:

- 800 ÷ 8 = 100  
- 400 ÷ 8 = 50  

So your Nether portal should be around:

X: 100  
Z: 50
